FREE SMILES

Give a smile. Get a smile.

Free Smiles is a fun little project to help people share smiles with the world around them. It's amazing how much of a difference it can make when you share a smile with a stranger, and everyone loves stickers!

Give them to servers and bartenders and convenience store cashiers. Give them to ticket-takers and flight attendants and grocery store baggers. Give them to bus drivers and bakers and pizza delivery drivers! Give them to your friends! Give them to your family! The more you give away, the more smiles will brighten your day.

Send us an email with your mailing address, and we'll send you some free smiles! :D
